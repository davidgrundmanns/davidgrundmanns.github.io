Welcome! I am a Research Associate at the Collaborative Research Center 884 “Political Economy of Reforms” and a PhD Candidate in Political Science at the Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences at the University of Mannheim. I am researching judicial politics and interdependent strategies of judicial and political actors. Making use of text as data for further statistical modeling is at the core of my methodological interests.

For the project C4 of the CRC 884, we are scaling the German Federal Constitutional Court on a unidimensional latent policy space utilizing Bayesian ideal point estimation.
